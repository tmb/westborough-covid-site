We, the students, need to speak up.

Westborough Public Schools is making a decision that will affect our day-to-day lives—let's make sure they hear what we have to say.

The Westborough Public Schools have created a plan to send us, the students, back to school next year—but they don't know what we think. You might’ve heard about this plan from your parents, from your friends—yet the district hasn't directly contacted us or asked our opinion. So, now is our time to be heard. We’re writing a letter to the School Committee with our thoughts.

The school is submitting their initial plan to the state today (July 31st), so please respond ASAP.

How you can engage:

1. Rank from 1 to 5

We've created a list of agree-disagree statements to poll your opinion on the school's plan. All you need to do is rank each statement based on how much you agree from 1 → 5. We'll compile the results of this poll into our letter to the School Committee. It should take under 2 minutes.
